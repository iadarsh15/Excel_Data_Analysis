Requirement::
This is a Road Accident Data Analysis and we have some insights from the data based on the below requirements-

Primary KPI (Key Performance Indicator):
	
Total casualties taken place after the accident.

Primary KPI: Total casualties and percentage of total with respect to accident severity and maximum casualties by type of vehicle.

Secondary KPI:     1. Total casualties with respect to vehicle type.
		   2. Monthly trend showing comparison of casualties for Current Year and Previous Year.
		   3. Maximum casualties by Road Type.
		   4. Distribution of total casualties by Road Surface.
		   5. Relation between casualties by Area/Location and by Day/Night.
